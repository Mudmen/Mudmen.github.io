Playground

在Playground里编写代码，编译器能立马在右边的侧边栏里给出你代码的结果 根据苹果的文档来看

When you are satisfied with your results of your experiments, you can copy the code from the playground and paste it into the source files of your iOS and OS X projects. 是说Playground就是用来检验代码的，你觉得代码OK了 就可以从Playground里把代码粘贴到你的源代码文件里去了。
